## Layout (Top to Bottom)

### Header
- **Title:** "RLCS SSA" (centered, prominent)
- **Global Search Bar:** directly below the title

### Main Content (Two Columns)

**Left Column — Navigation Buttons (stacked vertically):**
1. Head to Head
2. Top Performing Teams & Players *(combined — no need for separate buttons)*

**Right Column:**
- RLCS Standings box

### Featured Players
- 6 featured player cards in a horizontal row (each links to that player's profile)
- Profile search bar below the cards
