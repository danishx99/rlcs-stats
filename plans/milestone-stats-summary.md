# What we added to RLCS Stats

**1. New stats**
Six new ways to rank players, in a new "Milestones" section of the stat picker:
- **Games Played** — total games played.
- **Hat-tricks** — games with 3+ goals.
- **Saviours** — games with 3+ saves.
- **Exterminations** — games with 7+ demos.
- **MVPs** — games won as the team's top scorer.
- **OT Games** — games that went to overtime.
 
Available on the Stat Page, Event Page, and Compare Page. Hidden in Teams view.

**2. Arena filter**
A searchable dropdown to narrow leaderboards to a single map (e.g. Champions Field). Appears on the Stat Page and Event Page.
